Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CryptoMood Dash ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information when you use our website at cryptomood-dash.vercel.app (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. By using CryptoMood Dash, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

CryptoMood Dash is designed to be a privacy-respecting tool. We do not require user accounts, and we do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit CryptoMood Dash, the following non-personal information may be automatically collected through standard web technologies:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Screen resolution
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level, derived from IP address)

2.2 Voluntarily Provided Information

When you click the "Check Sentiment" button, the following data may be sent to our data collection endpoint (Google Sheets):

  • Current Fear & Greed Index value and label
  • Your screen dimensions
  • Browser user agent string
  • Referring page URL
  • Timestamp of the action

This data is used solely for aggregate analytics to understand how our dashboard is used. No personally identifiable information is collected through this mechanism.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

3.1 Our Cookies

CryptoMood Dash uses browser localStorage and sessionStorage to maintain a local visitor counter. This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

3.2 Third-Party Cookies

Our third-party service providers may set their own cookies on your device:

  • Google AdSense: May use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings.
  • Google Analytics: May use cookies to collect anonymized usage statistics. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Adsterra: May use cookies for ad delivery and performance measurement.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may not affect the core functionality of CryptoMood Dash, but may affect ad-related features.

4. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the CryptoMood Dash service
  • To understand how visitors use our dashboard (aggregate analytics)
  • To improve the user experience and add new features
  • To display relevant advertisements that support the free operation of the service
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

5. Third-Party Services

CryptoMood Dash integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Alternative.me API — provides Fear & Greed Index data. Privacy Policy
  • CoinGecko API — provides cryptocurrency price data. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — advertising service. Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — website analytics. Privacy Policy
  • Adsterra — advertising network. Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — website hosting. Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

Aggregate analytics data collected through the "Check Sentiment" feature is retained in Google Sheets for analytical purposes. This data does not contain personally identifiable information. Local storage data (visitor counter) is stored on your device and persists until you clear your browser data. We do not maintain user databases, profiles, or any form of persistent user accounts.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about what data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request that we delete any data associated with your usage.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or the links provided above.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured format.

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, most of these rights are automatically satisfied. If you have specific concerns, please contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

CryptoMood Dash is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Cryptocurrency investment and trading carries significant financial risk and is not appropriate for minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has accessed this service, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of CryptoMood Dash after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can reach us through our GitHub Issues page.
