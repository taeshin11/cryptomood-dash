About CryptoMood Dash

What Is CryptoMood Dash?

CryptoMood Dash is a free, real-time cryptocurrency market sentiment dashboard designed to give investors and traders a clear, instant snapshot of market conditions. It combines the widely followed Crypto Fear & Greed Index with live price data for the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization — all on a single, fast-loading page.

The cryptocurrency market is notoriously volatile, and emotional decision-making is one of the primary reasons traders lose money. When Bitcoin drops 10% in a day, panic spreads. When it surges, euphoria takes over. The Fear & Greed Index quantifies these emotions on a scale of 0 to 100, helping you separate signal from noise. CryptoMood Dash puts this critical data front and center, alongside the price movements that matter most.

Unlike bloated crypto platforms that overwhelm you with hundreds of metrics, CryptoMood Dash focuses on what experienced traders actually check multiple times per day: market sentiment and top coin prices. Nothing more, nothing less.

The Problem We Solve

Crypto investors face a fragmented information landscape. To check market sentiment, you visit one website. To check Bitcoin's price, you open another. To see altcoin performance, you load yet another tab. Each of these sites is cluttered with ads, pop-ups, and irrelevant data that slow you down.

CryptoMood Dash solves this by consolidating the two most critical pieces of market intelligence into one clean, distraction-free interface:

  • Fear & Greed Index — Is the market fearful or greedy right now? What was it yesterday, last week, and last month?
  • Top 10 Coin Prices — What are Bitcoin, Ethereum, and the other top coins trading at? Are they up or down in the last 24 hours?
  • 7-Day Sparklines — Mini price charts that show the weekly trend at a glance, without needing to open a charting tool.

The result is a dashboard you can check in under 5 seconds — ideal for the crypto investor who checks the market multiple times per day but doesn't want to get lost in data overload.

How It Works — The Technology

CryptoMood Dash is built as a lightweight, static web application with zero server-side dependencies. This architectural decision ensures maximum speed and reliability:

  • Fear & Greed Data — sourced from the Alternative.me API, which aggregates data from volatility (25%), market momentum/volume (25%), social media sentiment (15%), surveys (15%), Bitcoin dominance (10%), and Google Trends (10%).
  • Cryptocurrency Prices — sourced from the CoinGecko API, one of the most trusted crypto data aggregators, providing prices from hundreds of exchanges worldwide.
  • Gauge Visualization — the semicircular gauge is rendered using HTML5 Canvas with smooth animations, providing an intuitive visual representation of market sentiment from Extreme Fear (red) to Extreme Greed (green).
  • Auto-Refresh — the Fear & Greed Index updates every 5 minutes, and coin prices refresh every 60 seconds, ensuring you always see current data.
  • Responsive Design — built with Tailwind CSS and a mobile-first approach, the dashboard works flawlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Because CryptoMood Dash is entirely client-side, there are no servers to go down, no databases to maintain, and no login required. Open the page and the data is right there.

Understanding the Fear & Greed Index

The Crypto Fear & Greed Index is a composite metric that measures the overall emotional state of the cryptocurrency market. It ranges from 0 (Extreme Fear) to 100 (Extreme Greed) and is calculated daily using six weighted factors:

Factor Weight What It Measures
Volatility25%Unusual price swings compared to 30/90-day averages
Market Momentum25%Volume and momentum relative to recent averages
Social Media15%Sentiment analysis of crypto-related posts
Surveys15%Weekly polls of crypto investors
Bitcoin Dominance10%BTC market share (rising = fear, falling = greed)
Google Trends10%Search volume for crypto-related terms
