What Is the Crypto Fear & Greed Index?

The Crypto Fear & Greed Index measures the current sentiment of the cryptocurrency market on a scale from 0 (Extreme Fear) to 100 (Extreme Greed). It analyzes volatility, market momentum, social media, surveys, Bitcoin dominance, and Google Trends to produce a single number that tells you whether traders are feeling fearful or greedy today.

Historically, Extreme Fear can signal a buying opportunity — prices may be oversold. Extreme Greed suggests caution — the market may be due for a correction. Smart investors use the Fear & Greed Index alongside price data to make better-informed decisions.
